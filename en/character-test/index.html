Character Test

For new players, it is sometimes difficult to choose and define the first character among all the classes, races and background options available. So just answer these few questions and find out what could be the character that best fits your desires. If you do not know what to answer to a question, choose an answer followed by a *, which corresponds to the most common D&D options (those present in the Basic Rules).
